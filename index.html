Privacy Policy for Enhansera

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Enhansera respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information, including microphone access and related data used to provide the app’s features.

Information We Collect

When you use Enhansera, we may collect or process:

  • Audio provided by you through the microphone when you actively use audio-related features
  • Basic technical and usage information needed to operate, maintain, and improve app functionality

How We Use Information

We use this information only to:

  • Provide audio-related features requested by the user
  • Process user actions inside the app
  • Maintain, improve, and troubleshoot app performance

Microphone Permission

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Enhansera requests microphone access only when you actively use a feature that requires audio input. The app does not access the microphone in the background without your action.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share personal data with third parties except when necessary to provide the app’s core functionality, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security and integrity of the app.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the requested service, resolve technical issues, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our policies.

Children’s Privacy

Enhansera is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent where required by applicable law.

Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Your Choices

You can choose not to grant microphone permission, but some audio-related features of the app may not function properly without it.
